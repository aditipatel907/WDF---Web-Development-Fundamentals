FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What are the eligibility criteria for undergraduate programs?
    A: Applicants must have completed their higher secondary education (Class 12 or equivalent) with the required minimum marks in relevant subjects. Specific requirements vary by program and are listed in the admissions brochure.

Q: How can I apply for admission?
    A: You can apply online through our official website by filling out the application form and submitting the required documents.

Q: What is the application deadline?
    A: The application deadline for undergraduate programs is typically in the month of June. Please check the admissions page for specific dates.

Q: Are there any scholarships available?
    A: Yes, we offer a range of scholarships based on academic merit, financial need, and extracurricular.

Q: How can I find information about scholarships?
    A: Information about scholarships, including eligibility criteria and application process, can be found on the scholarships page of our website.
